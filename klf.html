Instructions to join our digital meeting



Step #1 Download Zoom
Zoom is real easy to use. The download link should take you straight to the app store if you're on your phone. It's also available to use on a computer or laptop.


Download Zoom
Step #2 Click Meeting Link
Once you've got Zoom installed on either your phone or computer, this link will take you straight into the Keep Leaning Forward meeting. The meetings will start about 5 minutes before noon, but you are able to join before and once it's started, you'll go into the meeting with the rest of us.
Join Meeting

Step #3: Follow Prompts to enable mic and camera.
It's fairly straightforward from this point. Just click to enable your microphone and camera so we can see and hear you. If you have issues, check out some of the video tutorials on Zoom's site.


Video Tutorials
Step #4: Meeting Etiquette
Nothing to it now: sit back and have an AA meeting from the comfort of your favorite chair or carseat. You should be able to swipe left to change the views and see multiple members of the meeting at once. The app automatically focuses in on whoever is speaking if you have it in the standard view of one person filling most of the screen. Note that if your camera is on, anybody in the meeting can see you regardless of what view you've got.
